Host your next event in our beautiful historic church or parish hall.

The Church

A Historic Setting for Special Occasions

Our beautiful Gothic Revival stone church, built in the 19th century, offers a stunning and reverent setting for weddings, concerts, memorial services, and other special events.

The church features beautiful stained glass windows, carved woodwork, and a peaceful sanctuary that seats approximately 150 guests. The space includes a pipe organ and piano, making it ideal for musical events.

  • Seating capacity: approximately 200
  • Pipe organ and steinway grand piano available
  • Wheelchair accessible entrance
  • Ideal for weddings, concerts, and ceremonies
  • full sound system for concerts or recitals.

The Parish Hall

A Versatile Space for Any Event

Our parish hall is a flexible, welcoming space perfect for receptions, meetings, community gatherings, workshops, and celebrations.

The hall includes a fully equipped kitchen, tables and chairs, and plenty of room for a wide range of events. Whether you're planning a birthday party, a reception, a community meeting, or a workshop, our hall can accommodate your needs.

  • Full kitchen facilities
  • Tables and chairs included
  • Suitable for receptions, meetings, and parties
