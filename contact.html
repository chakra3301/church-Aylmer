First Visit

Planning Your Visit

What to Wear

Come as you are! Our congregation dresses casually to semi-formally. There's no dress code — just wear whatever makes you comfortable.

When to Arrive

We suggest arriving about 10 minutes before the service begins. This gives you time to find a seat and receive a bulletin (the order of service) from one of our greeters.

What to Expect

A typical Sunday service lasts about one hour. You'll participate in hymns, prayers, scripture readings, a sermon, and Holy Communion. Don't worry if you're not familiar with the liturgy — the bulletin guides you through, and you can simply follow along with those around you.

After the service, we invite you to join us for coffee and fellowship in the parish hall. It's a great opportunity to meet people and ask questions.

Accessibility

Our building has wheelchair access via the main entrance. Large print bulletins are available upon request. Hearing assistance devices can be arranged with advance notice. If you have specific accessibility needs, please contact us before your visit so we can ensure you're comfortably accommodated.

Children & Families

Children are always welcome in worship! We have children's activity bags available, and little ones are free to move around as needed. Ask a greeter about our children's ministry programs.

Send Us a Message

Have a question? Want to learn more about our parish? We'd love to hear from you.

Whether you're planning your first visit, interested in getting more involved, or just want to say hello, please don't hesitate to reach out. Our parish administrator typically responds within 1-2 business days.

You can also call us during office hours or simply show up on Sunday morning — we're always happy to welcome visitors!
