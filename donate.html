Ways to Donate

There are several convenient ways to support Christ Church Aylmer.

CanadaHelps

Make a secure one-time or recurring donation online through CanadaHelps. You will receive a tax receipt automatically.

Donate via CanadaHelps

Interac e-Transfer

Send an Interac e-Transfer through your online banking to:

christchurchaylmer@gmail.com

Please include your name and address in the message field so we can issue a tax receipt.

Pre-Authorized Remittance

PAR is a convenient way to make regular monthly donations automatically from your bank account. It's managed through the Anglican Diocese of Ottawa.

Download the PAR brochure and form below to get started.

PAR Brochure & Form (PDF)

Your Gifts Make a Difference

Your financial contributions support the ongoing ministry and mission of Christ Church Aylmer, including:

  • Sunday worship services and music ministry
  • Building maintenance and preservation of our historic church
  • Community outreach and support programs
  • Contributions to the Aylmer Food Centre
  • Pastoral care and parish programs

Christ Church Aylmer is a registered charity. Charitable registration number: 108084658RR0103. Tax receipts are issued for all eligible donations.
