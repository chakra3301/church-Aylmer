Welcome

All Are Welcome Here

Christ Church Aylmer is a parish of the Anglican Diocese of Ottawa, part of the Anglican Church of Canada. We are a community of faith rooted in the Anglican tradition, committed to worship, fellowship, and service.

Whether you're exploring faith for the first time, returning after time away, or looking for a new church home, you'll find a warm welcome here. Come as you are — we're glad you're here.
